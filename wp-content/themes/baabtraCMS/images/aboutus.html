About us

An IT training company in Calicut, BAABTRA, works as a link in identifying, training and placing quality freshers from regional colleges and institutes. A firm that specialises in identifying, training and placing skilled resources from districts such as Kozhikode, Kannur, Malappuram, Wayanad and Kasargode for IT jobs in Calicut, Cochin, Trivandrum, Bangalore and middle eastern cities, BAABTRA provides training in technologies such as Android, PHP and Python in a totally industry oriented manner. We teach you to join companies; we let companies interact with the talent pool in domain-sensitive ways from six offices in Calicut connected to our Bangalore office as well.
